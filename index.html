Why Andafar

Field GIS data correction was a headache

GIS data collected in the field arrives at the office incomplete, unverified, and improperly linked. Without a dedicated tool, coordination between agents and supervisors means time-consuming manual work.

  • Excel files shared over WhatsApp, conflicting versions, no traceability
  • Field agents without connectivity - work impossible without a network
  • No link between the map feature and its correction form
  • Manual supervisor validation - no diff, no history, no formal rejections

With Andafar

  • Instant attribute join after corrections - no need to re-join through QGIS or ArcGIS manually
  • Offline-first: agents collect and correct data without any network, sync when back online
  • Each map feature is directly linked to its correction form - one tap to open, fill, and submit
  • Built-in before/after diff, full history, and formal approval or rejection workflow
  • Live validation or rejection - corrections are addressed on the spot, eliminating costly back-and-forth trips to the field
  • Daily corrections monitored per agent - supervisors see who submitted what, who reviewed it, and track team progress in real time

Features

Everything your field team needs

A complete stack built for real field conditions: intermittent connectivity, non-technical agents, complex forms, rigorous validation.

01
Offline-first, always available
Layers, features, forms and basemaps cached locally. Your agents work without connectivity; corrections sync when back online. Automatic conflict resolution.
50 features cached 3 local layers MBTiles basemap Sync file queue
02
Real-time notifications
Every submitted correction notifies the supervisor instantly. The agent receives approval or rejection as a push notification, in real time.
03
QR Code to Feature
Scan a QR on a marker to instantly identify the feature, navigate to it, and open the pre-filled form. Zero manual entry.
04
GPS Navigation
Turn-by-turn navigation to any GIS feature. Real-time distance and bearing via Google Maps SDK. Works with offline basemap.
05
Zones & Teams with granular control
Draw geographic zones on the map, assign agents by zone, configure supervisors by perimeter. Six roles: Owner, Admin, Supervisor, Corrector, Editor, Reader. Daily agent leaderboard to keep motivation high.

Process

From field to validation in 6 steps

A structured collaborative workflow between field agents, supervisors and GIS managers.

01

Import GIS Data

Load your GIS layers (GeoJSON, SHP, GPKG) and link your KoboToolbox forms. Up and running in minutes.

02

Configure Layers

Set up your GIS layers, define attribute fields and customise forms for each field agent.

03

Surveyors, Additions & Assignment Zones

Add field surveyors, define their coverage zones and assign features to each agent.

04

Field Correction

The agent locates the feature, scans the QR code, fills the form with photos, GPS and attribute data. Works fully offline.

05

Supervision & Validation

The supervisor sees the before/after diff in real-time from the web. They approve or reject with a comment. The agent is notified on mobile.

06

Export & QGIS

Export to GeoJSON, CSV, GPKG or SHP. Live QGIS sync via PostGIS connection or REST API.
